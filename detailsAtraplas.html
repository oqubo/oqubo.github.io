Atraplas, clean the oceans

Video game

Atraplas

Atraplás is an octopus that takes care of the planet.

Help him collect all the trash he finds in the water and join the clean oceans challenge. Can you tell which objects don't belong on the seabed?

Catch the drifting trash

Batteries, plastic rings, bags... even diapers! In Atraplás's sea, there's a lot of trash that we don't see from the surface. Fish out as much as you can with him and learn how many years it would take to disappear if no one removes it.

Respect marine life

But be careful! Atraplás doesn't want to disturb the animals living in the sea. If he accidentally catches one, he gets distressed and releases ink. Sponge will help you see the seabed again, but you must reward him by collecting the pearls you see in the current.
